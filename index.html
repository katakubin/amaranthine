Amaranthine

A Game by : Katakubin (Akhmad Redha P)

Welcome to Amaranthine! For now, we're in early development. Have fun & enjoy the game!

Amaranthine (am·a·ran·thine; ăm′ə-răn′thĭn) is a text-based game that automatically generate the adventure. The Goal is simple: either you archive 2000 points of Distance, or get 10 Amalgam. For the game itself, we're trying our best to make it as simple as possible yet still having an improvement by doing so.

For more information about our game (or other stuff), check out our changelog (it's below the game!). Also, if you're intrested in developing the game (yes! it's free to use!), check out our Github. For those who like to learn more about the game (or even the logic of it), just leave us a message
